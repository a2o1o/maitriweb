Controlled pilot with 30 participants showed early positive signals: 66.7% felt supported, 100% said they would return. Non-therapeutic and non-prescriptive by design.

Methodology

What we measure and how

We track three distinct layers: inputs (sessions conducted, participation), outputs (scholarships awarded, internships placed, jobs secured), and early signals (comfort, clarity, confidence from post-use surveys). We do not conflate correlation with causation.
