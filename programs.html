Core programs

Three interconnected pillars that support the full transition.
Core

Pehchaan

Structured sessions on communication and identity-building: voice, articulation, self-awareness, and storytelling. Girls learn to name what they want before being asked to pursue it.

Mentorship

Layered sisterhood

Core girls, peer mentors, senior mentors, and a leadership team. Every mentor is trained. The community provides continuity across school, college, and early work life.

Modules

Skill tracks

Interview preparation, resume basics, professional etiquette, goal-setting frameworks, and future literacy modules aligned to real constraints and aspirations.

Community

Sisterhood model

Shared groups, celebration of wins, alumni network, and continuity across transitions. The community is the infrastructure: girls support each other beyond any single program cycle.
