What is Maitri

Delhi government school girls

Maitri is a leadership platform and sisterhood network: a bridge from school to work that expands ambition, builds voice, and opens credible pathways through structured mentoring and long-term community support.

Confidence before competence Identity before ambition Voice before visibility Community before competition
Vision

A future where every girl's ambition is matched by access and strengthened by sisterhood.

Mission

To cultivate the first generation of young women leaders in government schools by expanding ambition, social capital, and structured access at life's critical transitions.

What Maitri does

A long-term ecosystem that builds identity, voice, and pathways, not one-off workshops.
School to work transition support Peer mentoring anchored in sisterhood Credible pathways through partners and scholarships
Core program

Pehchaan

Structured sessions on communication, self-awareness, and articulation to build confidence before competence.

Mentorship

Layered sisterhood

Seniors mentor juniors; mentors are trained; community continuity supports school, college, and early work transitions.

Opportunities

Credible pathways

Partnerships for internships, jobs, exposure, and scholarships, aligned to real constraints and context.
